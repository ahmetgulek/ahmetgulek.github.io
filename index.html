Ahmet Gulek

Postdoc in Economics

Stanford Immigration Policy Lab

I am a labor economist with additional interests in applied econometrics, development economics, spatial economics, and health economics. I received my PhD in Economics from MIT in May 2025. Starting in September, I will be a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Stanford Immigration Policy Lab with Jens Heinmueller and Ran Abramitzky. I will be on the 2026-2027 job market.
